Why Do We Procrastinate?

Overwhelm

Tasks seem too big or complex

Solution:

Break tasks into smaller, manageable chunks

Perfectionism

Fear of not meeting high standards

Solution:

Focus on progress, not perfection

Low Motivation

Lack of interest or energy

Solution:

Set small, achievable goals to build momentum

Effective Ways to Beat Procrastination

The 5-Minute Rule

Commit to working on a task for just 5 minutes. Once you start, you'll likely want to continue.

The Eisenhower Matrix

Organize tasks by urgency and importance to prioritize effectively.

Time Blocking

Dedicate specific time blocks to tasks, treating them as important appointments.
